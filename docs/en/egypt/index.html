Why Travelers Choose Egypt

🤿

Diving & Snorkeling

The Red Sea — world's best diving. Coral reefs, shipwrecks. Visibility up to 30m.

🏛

Ancient History

Pyramids of Giza — only surviving wonder. Luxor — world's largest open-air museum.

☀️

Year-Round

+22–35°C all year. Swimming season 12 months.

💰

Affordable

All Inclusive from $35/night. Restaurant dinner from $5. 2–3x cheaper than European resorts.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family-Friendly

Most hotels are family-oriented. Shallow sandy beaches. Russian animation.

Best Time to Visit Egypt

☀️

High Season (Oct–Apr)

+22–28°C

Perfect weather. Maximum prices. Book 3–4 months ahead.

🔥

Summer (May–Sep)

+30–40°C

Very hot. Prices drop 40–50%.

Practical Information

🛂 Visa

Visa on arrival for Russian citizens ($25). E-visa at visa2egypt.gov.eg.

💱 Currency

Egyptian Pound (EGP). USD accepted everywhere.

🗣 Language

Arabic. Russian & English in tourist areas.

🚌 Transport

Taxi (Uber/Careem in Cairo), minibuses, domestic flights.

🛡 Safety

Tourist areas safe. Don't leave resort zones alone. Drink bottled water.

📶 Internet & SIM

Wi-Fi in hotels. Local SIM from $5 with 10 GB.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa?

Russian citizens: visa on arrival ($25). Sinai: 15-day visa-free.

When to visit?

October–April: high season. Summer hot but 40% cheaper.

Which resort?

Sharm El Sheikh — diving. Hurghada — beaches. Marsa Alam — seclusion.

How much?

Economy: from $600. Comfort: from $1 200. Premium: from $2 500. Estimates.

Is Egypt safe?

Safe on resorts. Drink bottled water.

What to see?

Pyramids, Luxor, Valley of the Kings, Alexandria, St. Catherine's.

Vaccinations?

Not required. Hepatitis A recommended.
