Best Time to Visit UAE

☀️

High Season (Nov–Mar)

+24–28°C

Perfect weather. Book 2–3 months ahead.

🌸

Shoulder (Apr, Oct)

+28–35°C

Comfortable, fewer tourists.

🔥

Summer (May–Sep)

+38–48°C

Extreme heat. Prices drop 50–70%.

Practical Information

🛂 Visa

Visa-free 90 days for Russian citizens. Passport 6+ months valid.

💱 Currency

UAE Dirham (AED). USD & cards accepted everywhere.

🗣 Language

Arabic (official). English everywhere. Russian in major hotels.

🚌 Transport

Dubai Metro, taxi (Careem/Uber), car rental from $35/day.

🛡 Safety

Very safe. Strict laws: alcohol in hotels, modest dress.

📶 Internet & SIM

Wi-Fi everywhere. Local SIM from $15 with 10 GB.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa?

Russian citizens: visa-free 90 days.

Which emirate?

Dubai — shopping. Abu Dhabi — culture. Sharjah — budget. Fujairah — diving.

When to visit?

November–March ideal. Summer hot but 50% cheaper.

How much?

Economy: from $800. Comfort: from $1 800. Premium: from $5 000. Estimates.

Is UAE safe?

Very safe. Respect strict laws.

What not to do?

Don't photograph locals. Alcohol in hotels only.

Good for kids?

Perfect. Water parks, zoos, kids' zones everywhere.
