Best Time to Visit Indonesia

☀️

Dry Season (Apr–Oct)

+27–32°C

Best time. Prices peak July–August.

🌧

Wet Season (Nov–Mar)

+26–30°C

Short showers. Prices 20–30% lower.

Practical Information

🛂 Visa

Visa on arrival (~$33). 30 days, extendable.

💱 Currency

Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). Count zeros carefully.

🗣 Language

Indonesian. English in Bali. Russian rare.

🚌 Transport

Scooter rental is primary. Taxi (Gojek, Grab).

🛡 Safety

Safe. Road safety important. Don't drink tap water.

📶 Internet & SIM

Wi-Fi in hotels. Local SIM (Telkomsel) from $5.

Frequently Asked Questions

Visa?

Visa on arrival, 30 days (~$33). Extendable.

When to visit Bali?

April–October dry season. November–March cheaper.

Which area?

Ubud — yoga. Kuta — surf. Seminyak — dining. Canggu — hipster.

How much?

Economy: from $800. Comfort: from $1 600. Premium: from $4 000.

Is Bali safe?

Very safe. Main risk: road traffic.

What to eat?

Nasi Goreng, Satay, Gado-Gado, Babi Guling.
