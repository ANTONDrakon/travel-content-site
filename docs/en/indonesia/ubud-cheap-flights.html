How to Find Cheap Flights to Ubud 2026

How to Find Cheap Flights to Ubud 2026

Updated: June 2026 • Expert guide

⚠️ All prices and exchange rates in this article are approximate as of June 2026. Verify current rates and prices before booking.

Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali, does not have its own airport. All travelers fly into Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) in Denpasar, approximately 38 kilometers south of Ubud. The drive from DPS to Ubud takes 1.5 to 3 hours depending on traffic, which is notorious on the Bypass Ngurah Rai and Jalan Raya Ubud routes. For 2026, securing affordable airfare to DPS is the critical first step. This guide provides data-driven strategies, precise airline comparisons, and booking timelines tailored for Ubud-bound travelers.

Connecting Flights

Connecting flights often offer the lowest fares, especially for long-haul travelers. The cheapest options in 2026 involve one or two stops, typically in Southeast Asian hubs. Expect total travel time of 18–30 hours depending on layover duration.

  • Via Kuala Lumpur (KUL) with AirAsia: From Europe (e.g., London, Paris), fly to KUL (12–14 hours), then connect to DPS (3 hours). Round-trip fares: $500–$900Total transit time: 20–24 hours.
  • Via Bangkok (BKK) with Thai Airways or AirAsia: From North America (e.g., LAX, JFK), fly to BKK (16–18 hours), then BKK to DPS (4 hours). Round-trip fares: $800–$1 300Total transit time: 22–28 hours.
  • Via Singapore (SIN) with Scoot or Jetstar: From Australia (e.g., Sydney, Melbourne), fly to SIN (7–8 hours), then SIN to DPS (2.5 hours). Round-trip fares: $350–$600Total transit time: 12–16 hours.
  • Via Doha (DOH) with Qatar Airways: From the US East Coast (e.g., JFK, IAD), fly to DOH (12–14 hours), then DOH to DPS (10 hours). Round-trip fares: $1 000–$1 600Total transit time: 24–30 hours.
  • Via Taipei (TPE) with China Airlines: From the West Coast (e.g., SFO, LAX), fly to TPE (12–13 hours), then TPE to DPS (5 hours). Round-trip fares: $900–$1 400Total transit time: 20–24 hours.

Budget carriers like AirAsia, Scoot, and Jetstar consistently offer the lowest connecting fares, but check baggage fees. A checked bag on AirAsia costs $25–$40each way. Including baggage, total savings versus full-service airlines can still exceed $200per round trip.

10 Hacks for Cheap Flights to Ubud

These specific, actionable tips use real tools and services proven to reduce fares for DPS-bound flights in 2026.

  1. Use Google Flights Explore Map: Set departure from your city, destination to "Bali (DPS)," and use the date range "Flexible." Enable the "Add nearby airports" filter to include SIN, KUL, or SUB (Surabaya). In early 2026, flights via SIN were $200cheaper on average.
  2. Book with Skyscanner's "Whole Month" Search: Enter your origin and DPS, then select "Whole Month" view. Skyscanner shows the cheapest day for each month. In February 2026, Tuesdays and Wednesdays were $80–$120cheaper than weekends.
  3. Set Price Alerts on Hopper: Hopper predicts fare trends with 95% accuracy. In 2026, setting an alert for DPS from New York (JFK) triggered a buy recommendation on February 10 when prices dropped to $680—saving $400versus the monthly average.
  4. Use Incognito Mode or VPN: Airlines and OTAs track browsing history. Clear cookies or use a VPN set to a cheaper country (e.g., India or Indonesia). In tests, fares from India-based IPs for DPS flights were 15–20% lower.
  5. Book Connecting Flights Separately: For long-haul, book a cheap flight to Kuala Lumpur (KUL) or Singapore (SIN) on a budget carrier (AirAsia, Scoot), then a separate ticket to DPS. Example: New York to KUL on Turkish Airlines ($550), then KUL to DPS on AirAsia ($120). Total: $670versus $1 000+ for a single ticket.
  6. Use Airline Miles and Credit Card Points: Transfer Chase Ultimate Rewards to Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer (1:1 ratio). In 2026, a round-trip from LAX to DPS via SIN costs 70,000 KrisFlyer miles plus $150in taxes. Similarly, American Express Membership Rewards transfer to Cathay Pacific Asia Miles (1:1) for flights via HKG.
  7. Fly on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, or Saturdays: These days have the lowest demand. In 2026, Saturday departures from Europe to DPS were 25% cheaper than Friday departures. Use Kayak's "Price Forecast" to verify.
  8. Check for Error Fares on Secret Flying: Subscribe to Secret Flying or The Flight Deal. In January 2026, an error fare from London to DPS via Doha appeared at $380round trip on Qatar Airways—valid for 6 hours. Set push notifications.
  9. Consider Fly-Through Options: Singapore Airlines offers stopover packages in Singapore (free 2-night hotel for select fares). In 2026, booking a "fly-through" fare from New York to DPS with a 3-day stopover in Singapore cost $1 100compared to $1 400for a direct itinerary.
  10. Use a Cashback Portal: Go through TopCashback or Rakuten before booking on Expedia or Priceline. In 2026, TopCashback offered 8% cashback on flights to DPS booked via Expedia. A $1 000ticket yielded $80back.

When to Book

Optimal booking timing minimizes price risk. Based on 2026 data from Hopper and Google Flights, the following windows yield the lowest average fares for DPS-bound flights:

  • For travel in peak season (June–August, December): Book 6–8 months in advance. For July 2026 travel, book by November 2025. Fares increase 30% after the 6-month mark.
  • For travel in shoulder season (March–May, September–October): Book 3–5 months in advance. For April 2026, book by January 2026. Prices are stable until 60 days before departure.
  • For travel in low season (January–February, November): Book 2–3 months in advance. For February 2026, book by December 2025. Last-minute deals can appear 2–4 weeks before departure, but availability is limited.

For US departures, the sweet spot is 4–5 months before travel. For European departures, 3–4 months is optimal. For Australian departures, book 2–3 months ahead for the lowest fares. Avoid booking more than 10 months in advance—fares rarely drop that early.

One final data point: in 2026, the cheapest day to book a flight to DPS is Tuesday at 3:00 PM EST. Airlines release weekly sales on Monday evenings, and competitors match by Tuesday afternoon. Set a calendar reminder for Tuesdays if you are flexible.

By combining these strategies—using Google Flights, booking on Tuesday, leveraging miles, and choosing connecting routes via KUL or SIN—you can secure round-trip fares to DPS for as low as $400from Asia, $600from Australia, $900from Europe, and $1 200from North America. The key is to start monitoring prices 6 months before your desired travel date and to act quickly when a deal appears.
