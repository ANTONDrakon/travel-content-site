📱 IMUGA Declaration & QR Code for Maldives

Step-by-step guide:

  1. Fill out the IMUGA form at imuga.immigration.gov.mv within 96 hours before departure
  2. Enter your passport details, flight info, and accommodation address in Maldives
  3. Upload a photo of your passport and a selfie
  4. You will receive a QR code — save a screenshot on your phone
  5. Show the QR code at passport control upon arrival
  6. Also fill out the departure form within 96 hours before your return flight
  7. Important: without a completed IMUGA, you will not be allowed to board your flight to Maldives
  8. If you need help filling it out — our travel agent can assist you with the process

Practical Information

🛂 Visa

Free visa on arrival, 30 days. All nationalities.

💱 Currency

Maldivian Rufiyaa. USD everywhere. Cash on local islands.

🗣 Language

Dhivehi. English at all resorts.

🚌 Transport

Ferries, speedboats, seaplanes.

🛡 Safety

Very safe. Sunscreen essential. Don't touch coral.

📶 Internet & SIM

Wi-Fi at resorts. Local SIM from $10.

Frequently Asked Questions

Visa?

Free visa on arrival, 30 days, all citizens.

When to visit?

December–April dry season. May–November 30–50% cheaper.

How much?

Not budget. Economy: from $1 200. Comfort: from $2 500. Premium: from $7 000.

Budget option?

Yes, local islands: guesthouses from $25/night.

What to bring?

Sunscreen, reef shoes, UK-type G adapter.

Seaplane needed?

Only for remote resorts. Local islands by ferry.
