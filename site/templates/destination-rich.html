How to Get a QR Code for China

Step-by-step guide:

  1. Install WeChat or Alipay on your smartphone
  2. Register with your phone number — your Russian number works (SMS will arrive)
  3. In WeChat: search for "Health Kit" mini-program, fill out your data
  4. Upon arrival in China, show the QR code at passport control
  5. QR code must be updated daily — the app will remind you
  6. Keep a screenshot of the QR code on your phone (in case of no internet)
  7. Important: without a QR code, you will not be allowed into public places, restaurants, or shopping malls

Пошаговая инструкция:

  1. За 96 часов до вылета заполните форму IMUGA на сайте imuga.immigration.gov.mv

  6. Также заполните форму на выезд за 96 часов до обратного рейса
  7. Важно: без заполненной IMUGA вас не посадят на рейс в Мальдивы

📱 IMUGA Declaration & QR Code for Maldives

Step-by-step guide:

  1. Fill out the IMUGA form at imuga.immigration.gov.mv within 96 hours before departure
  2. Enter your passport details, flight info, and accommodation address in Maldives
  3. Upload a photo of your passport and a selfie
  4. You will receive a QR code — save a screenshot on your phone
  5. Show the QR code at passport control upon arrival
  6. Also fill out the departure form within 96 hours before your return flight
  7. Important: without a completed IMUGA, you will not be allowed to board your flight to Maldives
  8. If you need help filling it out — our travel agent can assist you with the process
